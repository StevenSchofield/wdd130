Bubbles!

Bubbles! is a 2d platformer game that requires you to jump from bubble to bubble to reach the end. It was made using the Godot game engine in the space of 72 hours for the Mini Jam 84. The theme we were given was Bubbles, with a limitation of "you are your own ammo." My goal was initially to use yourself as a projectile to attack enemies and break through walls, but it became more of a precision-platformer in the end. I will expand it to a full game in the future.

Current project - Labyrinth

Likely my most ambitious project to date, Labyrinth will be a top down roguelike, simmilar to the Binding of Issac. My sister has already provided much of the art, I have just been struggling to nail down the mechanics. The idea that inspired this game is
"pain is currency."
As you progress from room to room, you can purchace upgrades in exchange for your injuries, healing you in the process. Coming up with ideas for how to balance this idea has been tricky.
